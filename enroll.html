FAQ

Common questions

Do I need prior coding experience?
For AI Fundamentals — no. We teach Python from scratch. For Machine Learning and Neural Networks, you should be comfortable with basic Python and high-school math. Each course page lists exact prerequisites.
Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your billing period. No lock-in, no penalty.
How long do I have access to course material?
Pro subscribers have full access while subscribed. Free tier learners keep lifetime access to AI Fundamentals forever — even after cancelling Pro.
Are the certificates recognized by employers?
Our certificates are increasingly recognized by hiring managers at AI-forward companies. More importantly, the projects you build in each capstone are what employers actually look at — and they're designed to be portfolio-ready.
What's the time commitment per week?
Most learners spend 6–10 hours per week. Courses are self-paced, so you can slow down or accelerate based on your schedule. The weekly rhythm is designed around working professionals.
