Curriculum

10 weeks, algorithms to APIs

A complete journey from supervised learning fundamentals to a deployed, monitored ML service.

Week 1
Regression Deep Dive
Linear regression revisited, L1/L2 regularization, polynomial features, multicollinearity, and the bias-variance tradeoff.
Week 2
Classification Mastery
Logistic regression, softmax, class imbalance strategies (SMOTE, class weights), and calibrated probability outputs.
Week 3
Tree-Based Models
Decision trees, information gain, pruning, random forests, feature importance, and out-of-bag evaluation.
Week 4
Ensemble Methods
Bagging vs boosting, XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost, stacking, and blending — when and why to use each.
Week 5
SVMs & Kernel Methods
Hard/soft margin SVMs, the kernel trick, RBF and polynomial kernels, and multi-class classification strategies.
Week 6
Unsupervised Learning
K-means, DBSCAN, hierarchical clustering, Gaussian mixture models, and choosing the right algorithm for your data.
Week 7
Dimensionality Reduction
PCA with variance explained, t-SNE for visualization, UMAP for structure preservation, and practical applications.
Week 8
Feature Engineering
Encoding strategies, scaling, interaction terms, target encoding, automated feature selection, and leakage prevention.
Week 9
MLOps & Deployment
Model serialization, FastAPI endpoints, Dockerization, monitoring data drift, and CI/CD pipelines for ML.
Week 10
Capstone Project
End-to-end ML project: feature engineering, model selection, hyperparameter tuning, evaluation, and a live API.
