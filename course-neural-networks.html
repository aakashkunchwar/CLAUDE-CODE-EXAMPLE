Curriculum

12 weeks, perceptrons to LLMs

The most thorough deep learning curriculum available — built by researchers, for practitioners.

Week 1
Perceptrons & Backpropagation
Derivation of backprop through computational graphs, vanishing gradients, weight initialization strategies, and activations.
Week 2
Optimization & Regularization
SGD, momentum, Adam, RMSProp, learning rate schedules, dropout, batch normalization, and weight decay in practice.
Week 3
Convolutional Networks
Convolutions, pooling, receptive fields, ResNet, EfficientNet — building and training CNNs from scratch in PyTorch.
Week 4
Transfer Learning & Fine-tuning
Pre-trained vision models, feature extraction vs fine-tuning, domain adaptation, and data augmentation strategies.
Week 5
Sequence Models
RNNs, LSTMs, GRUs — training dynamics, vanishing gradients in practice, and sequence-to-sequence architectures.
Week 6
Attention & Transformers
Self-attention derivation, multi-head attention, positional encodings, and implementing the full Transformer paper.
Week 7
BERT & GPT Architectures
Pre-training objectives, masked language modeling, causal LM, scaling laws, and the anatomy of modern LLMs.
Week 8
LLM Fine-tuning
Instruction tuning, RLHF, LoRA, QLoRA, and parameter-efficient fine-tuning with Hugging Face and Axolotl.
Week 9
Diffusion Models
Score matching, DDPM, DDIM, latent diffusion — understanding and training Stable Diffusion from the ground up.
Week 10
Reinforcement Learning
MDPs, Q-learning, policy gradients, PPO — training game-playing agents with Gymnasium and your own environments.
Week 11
Production AI Systems
Model quantization, ONNX export, TensorRT optimization, Triton Inference Server, and observability in production.
Week 12
Capstone Project
Choose your path: fine-tune an LLM, build a computer vision system, or train a generative model — then deploy it.
